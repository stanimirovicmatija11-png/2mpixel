FAQ

What is the minimum purchase? +
The minimum block is 5×5 pixels = 25 pixels = $25 one-time or $25/month.
How does monthly subscription work? +
You pay for a minimum of 3 months upfront. After that, it's month-to-month. Cancel anytime and your pixels become available to others.
Can I choose where my pixels go? +
Small purchases (under 500px) are automatically placed in available spots. Purchases of 500px+ get free placement choice on the grid.
What image formats are accepted? +
PNG, JPG, GIF (static), WebP. Max file size 2MB. Your image will be scaled to fit your pixel block.
When does my ad go live? +
Automatically after payment clears — usually within minutes. All ads pass a basic content filter before appearing.
